Privacy & Cookies

This simple static site stores checklist progress only in your browser using localStorage. No personal data is sent to our server (there is no backend).

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (basic configuration) to understand aggregated usage (page views, language selection patterns). IP anonymization is handled by Google infrastructure. No custom user identifiers are set.

Advertising

This site displays a limited Google AdSense unit to help cover hosting/domain costs. Ads are loaded from Google and may use cookies or local storage subject to your regional settings. Blocking ads will not break core functionality.

Local Data Removal

Use the Reset button on the main page or clear your browser storage to remove all locally stored checklist progress.

Contact

If you have privacy concerns or wish to request removal of accidental data, open a GitHub issue in the project repository.
