Longpin

Found behind the pink wasp nest in the Shellwood area.

Weighted pin and haft, formed to pierce tough shells.

Curveclaw

Purchase from Mottled Skarr in the Hunter's March area for 140 Rosaries.

Arched sharpbone used by hunters to take down flying prey.

Curvesickle

Found in the room above the Far Fields Bellway after hitting all four targets with a single throw of the Curveclaw and then giving the Curveclaw to the bug. Rest at the nearest bench and then return to the target room to get the Curvesickle.

Radial sharpbone used by skilled hunters to take down dangerous prey.

Throwing Ring

Trigger the Trail's End Wish using the wishboard in Bellhart, then find Shakra in the most northeasterly part of Bilewater.

Hardened throwing weapon of the warrior Shakra. Blunt force weapon that rebounds between enemies and hard surfaces.

Pimpillo

Crafted at a table in the area above Yarnaby's bellhome in Greymoor. Requires one Craftmetal.

Fabric pouch filled with volatile material. Explodes on impact with enemies or when ignited by its fuse.

Conchcutter

Found in the Coral Tower cave in the Sands of Karak area.

Spiral weapon fashioned from a hardened conchspire. Thrown at an angle, it can ricochet and catch prey off-guard.

Silkshot I

Find the Ruined Tool in the southeastern part of Bilewater and then take it to Forge Daughter in the Deep Docks area.

Ancient weapon of the Weavers, repaired and modified by Forge Daughter. Uses shell canisters and the wielder's own Silk to fire.

Silkshot II

Find the Ruined Tool in the southeastern part of Bilewater and then take it to Twelfth Architect in the Underworks area.

Ancient weapon of the Weavers, repaired and modified by Twelfth Architect. Uses shell canisters and the wielder's own Silk to fire.

Silkshot III

Find the Ruined Tool in the southeastern part of Bilewater and then take it to the table near the bench in the northern part of Mount Fay.

Ancient weapon of the Weavers, repaired to its original form. Uses shell canisters and the wielder's own Silk to fire.

Delver's Drill

Found on a table in the southwestern part of the Underworks area.

Rotary tool used for boring into bone and rock. Drives the wielder downwards, while spinning the drill with destructive force.

Cogwork Wheel

Crafted by Twelfth Architect in the Underworks area. Requires one Craftmetal and 360 Rosaries.

Expanding circular blade powered by a cogwork motor. Propels forward, slicing through foes.

Cogfly

Crafted at a table in the High Falls area. Requires one Craftmetal.

Winged cogwork companion that will seek nearby foes and attack.

Rosary Cannon

Found on a table in the Forum area of High Halls.

Repurposed mechanism from the base of a rosary stringing machine. Rosaries must be inserted to reload the cannon.

Voltvessels

Found on a table in the northeastern part of the Memorium area during Act 3.

Convertible orbs containing electricity. The vessels can be thrown as a bola for an explosive discharge or staked as a spear to release a column of energy.

Flintslate

Found on a table in the Deep Docks area.

Sharpening slate made from cut and polished flintgem. Temporarily super-heats the needle's blade, imbuing each slash with flame and increasing lethality.

Snare Setter

Found on a table in the area above the bench in Weavenest Atla.

Trap-setting tool of the Weavers. Lays a Silk rune that ignites when foes come into contact. Uses shell canisters and the wielder's own Silk.

Flea Brew

Obtained as a reward for finding five fleas for Fleamaster Mooshka in The Marrow.

Invigorating beverage prepared by the Flea Caravan. Temporarily increases the speed of movement and attacks.

Plasmium Phial

Obtained as a reward for completing the Alchemist's Assistant Wish for Alchemist Zylotol in the Wormways area.

Spear-phial containing Plasmium. Injecting the liquid allows one to gain health beyond their natural limits.

Needle Phial

Given to players at the start of the Alchemist's Assistant Wish after speaking with Alchemist Zylotol in the Wormways area.

Tool designed to extract and store fluids. To pierce its target, the Needle Phial must first be charged to full tension.

Druid's Eye

Obtained as a reward for completing the Berry Picking Wish for Moss Druid in the Moss Home area.

Amulet of protection formed from Mossberries. As the bearer takes damage, the amulet weeps a green elixir that will form into Silk.

Druid's Eyes

Obtained as a reward for finding every possible Mossberry and giving them to Moss Druid in the Moss Home area during Act 3.

Powerful amulet of protection formed from many Mossberries. As the bearer takes damage, the amulet weeps a green elixir that will form into Silk.

Magma Bell

Crafted by Forge Daughter in the Deep Docks area. Requires one Craftmetal and 110 Rosaries.

Forged iron bell, crafted to ward against the intense heat of the Deep Docks. Reduces the damage taken from flame and magma.

Warding Bell

Found on a platform in the Far Fields area.

Holy bell inscribed with a hymn of protection. Shields the bearer as they bind their wounds.

Pollip Pouch

Obtained as a reward for completing the Rite of the Pollip Wish for Greyroot in the Shellwood area.

Pouch containing a fast-acting venom drawn from a flower's heart. Applies venom to equipped tools.

Fractured Mask

Purchase from Mottled Skarr in the Hunter's March area for 260 Rosaries.

Old bug's skull carved for protection. Shields against critical damage but shatters on impact. If shattered, the mask will be repaired while resting at a bench.

Multibinder

Purchase from Frey in the Bellheart area for 880 Rosaries after completing the My Missing Courier Wish.

Elaborate, etched Weaver spindle, usable only by wielders of Silk. Extends the act of binding, but increases its healing effect.

Weavelight

Obtained as a reward for beating the Moss Mothers boss fight in Weavenest Atla.

Gleaming apparatus of Weaver make. Its core emits a faint glow. Increases the speed of Silk regeneration.

Sawtooth Circlet

Crafted by Twelfth Architect in the Underworks area. Requires one Craftmetal and 230 Rosaries.

Flexible belt of serrated metal teeth, able to affix to the helm of a cloak. Flaring the cloak will spin the blades, damaging surrounding enemies.

Injector Band

Found on a table in the Whiteward area.

Brace of surgical pins from the Whiteward. Used to forcefully insert Silk deep inside a bug's shell. Increases the speed of binding.

Spool Extender

Purchase from Jubilana in Songclave for 720 Rosaries after completing the The Wandering Merchant Wish.

Silk-storing tool of the Citadel, fashioned after an artefact of the Weavers. Allows the storing of additional Silk beyond one's natural abilities.

Reserve Bind

Obtained as a reward for beating the Second Sentinel boss fight in the southeastern part of The Forum.

Ornate artefact of the Citadel, able to hold a Bind's worth of Silk. Used when healing is needed and one's natural resources run low. The Reserve Bind will be restored when resting at a bench.

Claw Mirror

Obtained as a reward for beating the Trobbio boss fight in the Stage area of the Whispering Vaults.

Theatrical tool of a vainglorious bug. When binding, emits a dazzling flash that damages surrounding enemies.

Claw Mirrors

Obtained as a reward for beating the Tormented Trobbio boss fight in the Stage area of the Whispering Vaults during Act 3.

Twin theatrical tools of a vainglorious bug. When binding, emits a searing blast that damages surrounding enemies.

Memory Crystal

Found behind a wall of ice in the Mount Fay area.

Reflective stone with mnemonic properties. When taking damage, a memory of the bearer is formed of crystal. The memory will shatter into sharp spines if an enemy comes into contact.

Snitch Pick

Purchase from Grindle in the Blasted Steps area for 740 Rosaries.

Hooked tool for the forceful retrieval of loot from bugs and husks. Harpoon into foes to steal rosaries and shell shards, while causing additional damage.

Volt Filament

Obtained as a reward for beating the Voltvyrm boss fight in the Voltnest area of Sands of Karak.

Charged organ from a defeated Voltvyrm. Can be coiled around thread, imbuing Silk Skills with electric charge.

Quick Sling

Found by breaking through a false ceiling in the Bilewater area.

Taut-drawing mechanism built from reed and bone, intended for mounting on the limb of a bug. Doubles the number of tools thrown.

Wreath of Purity

Found on a platform in the Putrified Ducts area.

Fragile band woven of reed and leaf, crafted to repel Muckmaggots. If broken, the wreath will be repaired while resting at a bench.

Longclaw

Talisman fashioned from the talon of a large predator. Increases the range of needle attacks.

Wispfire Lantern

Obtained as a reward for beating the Father of the Flame boss fight in the section of Bellheart to the west of Wisp Thicket.

Broken iron lantern containing smouldering wispfire. Wisps will spawn from the lantern and burn foes, at the cost of Silk.

Egg of Flealia

Obtained as a reward for completing the Lost Fleas Wish for Fleamaster Mooshka in The Marrow.

Icon of the mythical mother flea, passed down from caravans of old. A symbol of protection and kinship. Decreases the cost of Silk Skills while health is full.

Pin Badge

Obtained as a reward for beating the Pinstress boss fight in the Mount Fay area during Act 3.

Emblem of the Order of the Pinstress, the old blademasters of Pharloom. Reduces charge time of Needle Strike.

Compass

Purchase from Shakra for 70 Rosaries.

Intricate tracking device, carved from shell. When equipped, the bearer's location will be marked on their map.

Shard Pendant

Found on a table in The Marrow.

Crude trinket worn by collectors and crafters. Allows one to collect more shell shards from defeated creatures.

Magnetite Brooch

Purchase from Pebb in the Bonebottom area for 120 Rosaries.

Holy stone inscribed with a prayer for rosaries. Any loose beads will be pulled toward the wearer.

Weighted Belt

Purchase from Mort in the Pilgrim's Rest area of Far Fields for 160 Rosaries.

Heavy band favoured by those with an unsteady step. The wearer will recoil less when striking foes and will not be flung as far when taking damage.

Barbed Bracelet

Found on a bug corpse in Sinner's Road.

Vicious bracelet worn by those who believe they have not been duly punished. Increases damage taken, but also increases damage dealt by needle.

Dead Bug's Purse

Found on a bug corpse in the Wormways area.

Discarded purse of a fallen pilgrim. Allows the bearer to retain some rosaries upon defeat.

Shell Satchel

Found on a bug corpse in the Wormways area while playing the game on Steel Soul mode.

Quick access container formed from an old shell. Increases the quantity of crafted tools.

Magnetite Dice

Repeatedly beat Lumble the Lucky while playing the Dice mini-game.

Playthings carved from magnetic material. Used in simple games and believed to bring luck to the bearer.

Scuttlebrace

Crafted by Twelfth Architect in the Underworks area. Requires one Craftmetal and 140 Rosaries.

Artificial spine that clasps tight around the shell. Enhances the wearer's innate flexibility, allowing rapid retreat.

Ascendant's Grip

Purchase from Jubilana in Songclave for 350 Rosaries after completing the The Wandering Merchant Wish.

Gilded piton that allows one to hold in place while on a wall.

Spider Strings

Purchase from Jubilana in Songclave for 320 Rosaries after completing the The Lost Merchant Wish.

Sophisticated Weaver device. Resonates with music played to amplify and enhance a performance. Expands and strengthens the effects of Needolin.

Silkspeed Anklets

Obtained after sprinting across the chamber in the Weavenest Cindril area of Far Fields.

Experimental anklets of Weaver make. Draws upon the wearer's own Silk to increase sprinting speed.

Thief's Mark

Purchase from Grindle in the Blasted Steps area for 350 Rosaries.

Symbol worn to identify oneself to fellow thieves. Gain extra rosaries from defeated enemies, but lose some rosaries on taking damage.
